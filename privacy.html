Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Who We Are

GymGear Compare ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website gymgearcompare.com — a free, independent gym equipment, clothing, and supplement comparison tool. We are not affiliated with any brand or retailer we feature.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to operate the site:

  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, browser type, and device type, collected automatically via Google Analytics 4.
  • Email address — only if you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter or submit the sponsor contact form.
  • Cookies — small text files placed on your device by us or third parties (see Cookies section below).

We do not collect your name, payment information, or any sensitive personal data.

How We Use Your Information

  • To analyse site traffic and improve the user experience (Google Analytics)
  • To send deal alerts or updates if you subscribed (email only)
  • To respond to sponsor or partnership enquiries

We never sell your data to third parties.

Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • Google Analytics — tracks anonymous usage statistics (_ga, _gid). You can opt out via Google's opt-out tool.
  • Google AdSense — serves relevant ads and may use cookies to personalise them. You can manage ad preferences at adssettings.google.com.
  • Functionality cookies — remember your filter and sort preferences during a session.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Some site features may not work correctly without them.

Affiliate Links & Advertising

GymGear Compare participates in affiliate programmes including the Amazon Associates Programme. When you click a "Buy" button and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

Affiliate relationships never influence our rankings or comparison results. All verdicts are generated independently by AI based on specs, price, and customer ratings.

We also display third-party advertisements via Google AdSense. These advertisers may use cookies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing activity.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics — usage analytics
  • Google AdSense — advertising
  • Formspree — contact form
  • Amazon Associates — affiliate programme
  • Render — backend hosting
  • Vercel — frontend hosting

Data Retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months. Email addresses collected via the newsletter are retained until you unsubscribe. Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email we send
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at roei.karpik@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

GymGear Compare is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, email us at roei.karpik@gmail.com.
